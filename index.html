Jaehoon Jang

I am an AI researcher at Smilegate AI. I received my Master's degree from the 3D Vision & Robotics lab at UNIST, supervised by Prof. Kyungdon Joo. My primary research focuses on creating 3D worlds through neural rendering, implicit neural representations, and generative models, while extending these techniques to real-world applications in robotics and gaming.

I was a visiting scholar at Visual Computing Group, Harvard, supervised by Prof. Hanspeter Pfister, and working closely with Dr. Wanhua Li.
