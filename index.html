Jaehoon Jang

I'm a M.S student in 3D Vision & Robotics lab at UNIST, supervised by Prof. Kyungdon Joo. I am broadly interested in 3D computer vision and deep learning. Recently, my research focuses on creating 3D contents with neural rendering, implicit neural representations, and generative models.

I am now a visiting scholar at Visual Computing Group, Harvard, supervised by Prof. Hanspeter Pfister, and working closely with Dr. Wanhua Li.
